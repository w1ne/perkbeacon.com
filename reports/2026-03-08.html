  • Eligibility: Founded less than 10 years ago, New Render customer, AI tier requires at least $2.5M in funding from enrolled top-tier VCs ...

  • Google Cloud for Startups (Google)
  • Benefits: Up to $350,000 in Cloud credits for AI-first startups (Seed to Series A), Up to $200,000 in Cloud credits for Seed to Series A startups, $2,000 in credits to build an MVP ...
  • Eligibility: Seed to Series A startups for high-tier credits, MVP phase for entry-tier credits, AI-first startups get preferential credit caps
  • Link: https://cloud.google.com/startup
  • Cloudflare for Startups (Cloudflare)
  • Benefits: Up to $250,000 in Cloudflare credits for high-growth AI companies or Tier 1 VC/Accelerator network startups, Security and performance services on a global 280 Tbps network, Zero egress fee storage (R2) ...
  • Eligibility: Founded within the last 5 years, Funded up to Series B, Must have a software-based product/service and an active website ...
  • Link: https://www.cloudflare.com/forstartups/
  • Notion for Startups (Notion)
  • Benefits: 6 months free on the Business plan with Notion AI included (affiliated with select partners), 3 months free on the Business plan with Notion AI included (non-affiliated startups), 1 month free for SMBs or solo applicants ...
  • Eligibility: Non-paying Notion customer, Under 100 employees, Valid business website and company domain email (no generic providers like Gmail) ...
  • Link: https://www.notion.com/startups
  • Atlassian for Startups (Atlassian)
  • Benefits: 12 months free for up to 50 users on Jira Premium and Confluence Premium, Loom Business + AI free for up to 50 seats, Jira Product Discovery Premium (up to 10 creators) ...
  • Eligibility: Not an existing Atlassian paid customer, VC funded or associated with a partner accelerator/incubator, Raised less than $10 million in external funding
  • Link: https://www.atlassian.com/software/startups
  • MongoDB for Startups (MongoDB)
  • Benefits: MongoDB Atlas credits (amount unspecified in text), Access to Voyage AI tokens for advanced embedding models, Dedicated technical support and one-on-one expert sessions ...
  • Eligibility: Less than 7 years old, Series A or earlier funding stage, Software-based product (agencies not eligible) ...
  • Link: https://www.mongodb.com/solutions/startups
