# Daily Startup Perks Research - 2026-03-09

## Key Updates
- Google Cloud for Startups introduced high-value tiers offering up to $350,000 in credits for AI-first startups.
- AWS Activate now allows credits to be redeemed for third-party foundation models via Amazon Bedrock, including Anthropic and Meta.
- Intercom for Startups added with a 90% first-year discount and free AI Agent resolutions.
- Cloudflare for Startups confirmed credit tiers reaching $250,000 for high-growth AI companies.

## New Programs
- None

## Changes Detected
- **AWS Activate** (Amazon Web Services)
  - Benefits: Up to $100,000 in AWS Activate Credits for Portfolio startups, $1,000 in credits for self-funded Founders startups, Credits redeemable for third-party models (Anthropic, Meta, Mistral) on Amazon Bedrock ...
  - Eligibility: Founded within the last 10 years, Pre-Series B funding stage, Portfolio tier requires Org ID from an approved Activate Provider (VC/Accelerator) ...
  - Link: https://aws.amazon.com/startups
- **Google Cloud for Startups** (Google)
  - Benefits: Up to $350,000 in Cloud credits for AI-first startups over two years, Up to $200,000 in Cloud credits for Seed to Series A startups, $2,000 in credits for MVP development ...
  - Eligibility: Seed to Series A startups for high-tier credits, Must be new to Google Cloud for high-tier benefits, AI-first startups get preferential credit caps
  - Link: https://cloud.google.com/startup
- **Render for Startups** (Render)
  - Benefits: AI Tier: $100,000 in startup credits plus 1:1 onboarding, Scale Tier: $25,000 (funding >$1M) or $10,000 (funding <$1M) in credits, Build Tier: $5,000 in credits via partner referral ...
  - Eligibility: Founded less than 10 years ago, AI tier requires $2.5M+ funding from top-tier VCs, Prototype tier requires min $25K seed funding ...
  - Link: https://render.com/startups
- **HubSpot for Startups** (HubSpot)
  - Benefits: 90% off Year 1 (for Seed/Series A with partner affiliation or verified funding), 30% off Year 1 (for startups in entrepreneurial organizations), 50% off Year 2 and 25% off Year 3 ...
  - Eligibility: Pre-seed, Seed, or Series A funding status, Affiliated with approved partners (VCs, accelerators, etc.), Funding verified via Pitchbook or Crunchbase
  - Link: https://www.hubspot.com/startups
- **Cloudflare for Startups** (Cloudflare)
  - Benefits: Up to $250,000 in Cloudflare credits for Tier 1 VC network or AI startups, $5,000 to $100,000 credit tiers based on funding and partner affiliation, Zero egress fee storage (R2) and Workers AI ML model hosting ...
  - Eligibility: Founded within the last 5 years, Funded up to Series B, Software product or service with active website ...
  - Link: https://www.cloudflare.com/forstartups/
- **Atlassian for Startups** (Atlassian)
  - Benefits: 12 months free for up to 50 users on Jira Premium and Confluence Premium, Loom Business + AI free for up to 50 seats, Jira Product Discovery Premium (up to 10 creators) ...
  - Eligibility: Not an existing Atlassian paid customer, VC funded or associated with a partner accelerator/incubator, Raised less than $10 million in external funding
  - Link: https://www.atlassian.com/software/startups
- **Notion for Startups** (Notion)
  - Benefits: Up to 6 months free on Business plan with Notion AI (partnered), 3 months free on Business plan with Notion AI (non-partnered), Equivalent value up to $12,000 for up to 100 employees ...
  - Eligibility: Non-paying Notion customer, Under 100 employees, Company domain email required (no generic providers) ...
  - Link: https://www.notion.com/startups
- **GitHub for Startups** (GitHub)
  - Benefits: Up to 20 seats of GitHub Enterprise free for one year, Up to 20 seats of GitHub Advanced Security 50% off for one year, Access to exclusive startup network events and resources ...
  - Eligibility: Received outside equity funding (Series B or earlier), New to GitHub Enterprise or Advanced Security, Must use a subscription-based model (no pay-as-you-go)
  - Link: https://github.com/enterprise/startups
- **Brex Partner Perks** (Brex)
  - Benefits: $5,000 in AWS credits (new customers), $2,500 in OpenAI credits for 1 year, $500 in Anthropic API credits ...
  - Eligibility: Must be a Brex cardholder, Some perks limited to new customers of the partner service
  - Link: https://www.brex.com/support/brex-partner-perks
- **MongoDB for Startups** (MongoDB)
  - Benefits: Up to $5,000 in MongoDB Atlas credits (higher for AI companies), Access to Voyage AI tokens for high-performance retrieval, Dedicated expert technical session and 24/7 Developer Support (30 days free) ...
  - Eligibility: Less than 7 years old, Series A or earlier funding, New participants only ...
  - Link: https://www.mongodb.com/solutions/startups
- **Stripe Atlas** (Stripe)
  - Benefits: $2,500 in Stripe product credits for use in the first year, Over $50,000 in partner discounts (AWS, Mercury, etc.), Delaware C Corp or LLC incorporation and EIN filing ...
  - Eligibility: One-time setup fee of $500, Available to founders in over 140 countries
  - Link: https://stripe.com/en-fi/atlas
- **DigitalOcean Startups** (DigitalOcean)
  - Benefits: DigitalOcean credits for cloud infrastructure (amount varies), $1.90/GPU/hour on-demand GPU Droplets for 12 months, Free Standard-tier support for 15 months ...
  - Eligibility: Early-stage startups, Application required
  - Link: https://www.digitalocean.com/startups
